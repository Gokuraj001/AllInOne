1. INTRODUCTION

Introduction

Welcome to ALL-IN-ONE ("Website," "Service," "we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our Website.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Website and use our services, including our free tools for:

Image to PDF conversion

PDF to Office file conversion

Merge PDF files

Split PDF files

Compress PDF files

Link to QR code generation

Organize PDF documents

PDF to Office file conversion

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use our Website or services. This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws in India and international privacy standards.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information We Collect

2.1 What We Do NOT Collect

We respect your privacy and maintain a minimal data collection approach. We explicitly DO NOT collect:

Personal identification information (name, address, phone number, etc.)

Email addresses

File names or metadata from your uploaded documents

User location data (country, city, IP address, or geolocation)

Payment information (unless you opt for premium features)

Browsing history or detailed user behavior tracking

Personal device information (unless required by our services to function)

Cookies on your device

Any data that could personally identify you

2.2 What We Collect Through Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Website. Google Analytics collects the following non-personal information:

Session Information: Duration of your visit, pages visited, referral source

Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type

Traffic Data: Which tools/features you use, general usage patterns

Analytics Features: We utilize all standard Google Analytics features including audience demographics, interests, user acquisition data, behavior flow analysis, and conversion tracking

Important: Google Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous. It cannot be used to identify individual users.

2.3 Automatic Data Collection

When you visit our Website, we may automatically receive certain information through your browser or device:

Browser type and version

Operating system

Referring/exit pages

Pages visited and time spent on each page

General usage patterns

This information is collected automatically by Google Analytics and is not linked to any personal identification information.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through Google Analytics for the following purposes:

Service Improvement: To understand how our tools are being used and identify areas for improvement

User Experience: To optimize Website performance and user interface

Feature Development: To determine which features are most popular and develop new features accordingly

Website Maintenance: To ensure the Website functions properly and efficiently

Analytics: To generate reports and insights about Website usage patterns

Troubleshooting: To diagnose technical issues and problems

We DO NOT use your information for:

Targeted advertising or marketing

Selling or renting your data to third parties

Creating user profiles for commercial purposes

Tracking individual user behavior

Retargeting or remarketing campaigns

4. FILE UPLOADS & PROCESSING

File Uploads & Processing

4.1 Instant Deletion Policy

When you upload files to ALL-IN-ONE:

Your files are processed immediately for the requested conversion or operation

Your files are deleted instantly after processing is complete

We do not retain, store, or backup any of your files

We do not access, view, or analyze your file contents

Files are not used for any purpose other than the conversion you requested

4.2 Client-Side Processing

Whenever possible, file processing occurs on your device (client-side) rather than on our servers, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

4.3 File Privacy

Your uploaded files are:

Never shared with third parties

Never used for training, analysis, or any other purpose

Never retained for backup or recovery purposes

Completely private and confidential

Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about Website usage.

5.2 Google Analytics Data Collection

Google Analytics collects:

Anonymous usage statistics

Audience demographics and interests

Traffic sources and user acquisition data

Behavior flows and user journeys

Device and browser information

Note: We DO NOT use Google Analytics to track individual user behavior or collect personal information.

5.3 Google's Data Practices

Google may use the data collected to contextualize and personalize ads in Google's own advertising network

Google Analytics data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Google may transfer data to third countries in accordance with Google's standard contractual clauses

5.4 Opt-Out Options

You can prevent Google Analytics from collecting your data by:

Disabling cookies in your browser settings

Using privacy-focused browser extensions

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

6.1 No Cookies Used

We DO NOT use cookies on our Website. This means:

We do not store persistent data on your device

We do not track your activity across sessions

We do not use session cookies

Your browsing session does not leave any tracking data on your device

8. DATA RETENTION

Data Retention

8.1 No Data Retention Policy

We do not retain any user data. Specifically:

Files: All uploaded files are deleted immediately after processing

Personal Information: We do not collect personal information, so there is nothing to retain

Usage Data: Google Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention policies (typically 14 months by default)

Support Data: We do not process support requests that would require data retention

Server Logs: We do not retain server logs for personal information purposes

8.2 Google Analytics Retention

You can control how long Google Analytics retains your data by:

Requesting data deletion through Google's tools
